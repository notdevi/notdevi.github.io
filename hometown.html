ABOUT BALIKPAPAN

"Oil City"

My dear hometown Balikpapan is located in the province of East Kalimantan. It's the second largest city there after Samarinda. As a business and industrial center, Balikpapan has the largest economy in all of Kalimantan.

Today, Balikpapan has become a large, multi-ethnic city and is often named as one of the most livable cities in Indonesia.

BALIKPAPAN'S LANDMARKS

Here's 3 most famous landmarks in Balikpapan :

1

TUGU ADIPURA

The Adipura Monument was built as a form of pride because the City of Balikpapan has often won the Adipura award.

2

TUGU MONPERA

Monpera has a building whose foundation is like a trapezoid, then on it is a statue of three soldiers with excited expressions.

3

TUGU BERUANG MADU

The sun bear statue has a total height of 12 meters. The details are a 7 meter high statue of a honey bear made of cement.
